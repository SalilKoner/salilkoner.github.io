Brief biography

Welcome to my webpage! I will be starting as an Assistant Professor (tenure-track) in the Department of Statistics at North Carolina State University in the Fall of 2019.

I have a PhD in Statistics from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, and was very fortunate to work under the direction of my adviser Jan Hannig at UNC, and Curt Storlie at the Mayo Clinic.

Before my PhD I studied math and economics at Eastern Michigan University, and completed a master's degree in mathematics from New York University, Courant. I was very lucky to write a master's thesis under the direction of Ying Lu at NYU.
